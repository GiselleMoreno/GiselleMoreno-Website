Giselle Moreno

History/Education

Hello! My name is Giselle Moreno, and I am currently a second year History major with the intent to add on an Education Major. I plan on graduating from the University of California, Riverside, by the year 2025. After that, I hope to continue onto graduate school and potentially get a PHD in Administration.

I am interested in becoming an educator due to the influence of my parents. Both are educators themselves, and after shadowing them in their classrooms to get a hands on approach to teaching, I decided that being an educator was the right career move for me. I specifically want to become a high-school history teacher, combining my interest in history with my passion for helping others. I aim to focus on Chicano/U.S. History, as I am a member of both areas. As a Chicana student, I have experienced the lack of Latino studies in K-12 education, and so I want to advocate for my community. This will create more pride, while informing those who do not have direct access to our culture about who we are as people.

I have many volunteer experiences, from tutoring young elementary students in Math and English homework, to becoming a mentor for incoming freshman to the community. As a mentor to both groups, I have developed an understanding on how to approach teaching students who have difficulty in their classes, by tailoring plans specifically for them to succeed rather than sticking to what is not working.
